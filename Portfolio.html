About Me

Professional Story:

My journey as a designer is a blend of artistry and innovation. Starting in animation and 3D art, I’ve worked on projects like creating holograms for cutting-edge mixed reality and designing VR experiences that inspire positive change. As I advance my skills in UX design, I’m passionate about bridging the gap between technology and people by crafting intuitive and impactful experiences.

Design Philosophy:

I believe great design begins with understanding people’s needs and crafting effortless and engaging solutions. For me, design is about creating moments of clarity and connection—ensuring every interaction is as seamless as it is memorable. My work thrives on empathy, creativity, and a dedication to improving how users experience the world through technology.
